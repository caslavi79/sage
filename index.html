About Sage

Healing is personal

Liz Childress, licensed massage therapist, started Sage Therapeutics with a simple approach: listen first, then let the body guide the work.

Her background is in therapeutic massage, cupping, gua sha, and aromatherapy, but what sets Sage apart is the approach. She won't dig straight into the spot that hurts. If your lower back is locked up, the session starts by softening the muscles around it first, your hips, your sides, everything connected, so by the time the work reaches the source, your body is actually ready to let go. It takes patience, but it's why people leave feeling different than they expected.

How We Work

What we work with

Cupping Therapy

Suction cups on the skin increase blood flow, relieve muscle tension, and reduce inflammation. Looks more intense than it feels.

Gua Sha

A smooth-edged tool scraped along the skin to promote circulation, reduce inflammation, and relieve tension in the muscles.

Hot Stone Therapy

Heated stones promote deep relaxation and help release muscle tension. One of the most calming things you can do for your body.
