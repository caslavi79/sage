About Sage

Healing is personal

Every session at Sage begins with listening. Before any technique or modality, there's a conversation — about what your body is carrying, what you need relief from, and what healing looks like for you.

Liz Childress is a licensed massage therapist who founded Sage Therapeutics with a simple belief: that bodywork should be guided by intuition and care, not a timer. Her sessions blend therapeutic massage with modalities like cupping, gua sha, and aromatherapy — shaped entirely around what you need that day.

This isn't a spa assembly line. It's one person, one room, and the unhurried attention your body deserves.

How We Work

More than just a massage

Cupping Therapy

An ancient practice using gentle suction to increase blood flow, ease muscle tension, and reduce inflammation. Deeply relaxing and surprisingly restorative.

Gua Sha

A traditional healing technique that works through layers of tension using smooth-edged tools. Promotes circulation, relieves deep muscle tightness, and leaves you feeling renewed.

Hot Stone Therapy

Heated stones placed on key points melt through tension while the warmth penetrates deep into your muscles. Profoundly calming and effective for chronic tightness.
